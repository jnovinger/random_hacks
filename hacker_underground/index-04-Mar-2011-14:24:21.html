Hacker Underground is an event for developers at SXSW who want to be able to talk to anyone in the room about coding without having to worry about bumping in to a marketing "guru".


About the event:

  • Everyone who comes to Hacker Underground must have a hack
  • 20 hackers will be chosen at random during the event to present their hack.
  • If you do not teach two people in the room something new, you will be asked to leave.
  • After the presentations, we'll mingle and discuss our hacks with each other.

How to get in:

  • Solve the puzzle to register for the event.
  • If you want to make the event more exclusive or you thought the puzzle was too easy, tweet anything about #hackerunderground.
  • For every 5 people who tweet, we'll increase the difficulty of the puzzle (each puzzle will be up for at least two hours).
A hack can be code, shell tricks, a life hack, or generally anything that is useful and not common knowledge.
